Venom Command Protocols:
- "Look at this": Triggers Vision System (Florence-2).
- "Open [App Name]": Triggers App Launcher.
- "Search for [Query]": Triggers Google Search.
- "Learn" or "Scan Memory": Triggers Knowledge Ingestion Sequence.
- "System Status": Checks CPU/RAM/Battery.
- "Shutdown": Deactivates the Symbiote.
